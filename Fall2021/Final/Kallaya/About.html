Graphic Designer

Illustrator

I'm originally from Thailand. I have loved to draw since I was a child. I am now turning my hobby into my profession. I am especially interested in functional design that considers environmental conservation, reduced waste, and social justice.

I enjoy connecting with nature. Hiking and gardening are my favorite pastimes. With competencies in pottery, furniture design and watercolor illustration, I am able to work in a variety of media.

My work is a vehicle through which I express feelings, values and intentional messages to targeted audiences. One of my professional goals is to illustrate children's books.
