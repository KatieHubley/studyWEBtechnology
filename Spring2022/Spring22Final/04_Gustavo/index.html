Live, Love, & Skateboard!

Here at Champion Skates, we aspire people to go big or go home for skateboarding. We are a skate brand who teaches people how to skate, sell skateboards, and sell everything else that is part of a skateboarding. We currently have one store in Downtown, Silver Spring and a camp here as well. We own a camp that’s also titled as our brand name and that is where everyone goes to learn how to skate. It is like regular camping but for skateboarding. We strive for the best for everyone and make sure everyone has a good time. Everyone’s safety is our #1 priority. Don’t worry. You will have a fun time here.
